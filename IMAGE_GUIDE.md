# Image Guide for Dr Dhobi Website

## üé® Colorful Image Recommendations

To make your laundry service website more attractive and vibrant, replace black and white images with colorful, high-quality photos.

### Required Images

1. **Hero Image** (`/public/laundry.jpg`)
   - **Suggestion**: Bright, colorful image of freshly laundered clothes
   - **Colors**: Vibrant blues, whites, pastels
   - **Example**: Stack of neatly folded colorful clothes, or hanging shirts in various colors
   - **Size**: 600x450px (minimum)

2. **About Section Image** (`/public/laundry1.jpg`)
   - **Suggestion**: Happy laundry team members or modern laundry facility
   - **Colors**: Warm, inviting colors with good lighting
   - **Example**: Staff sorting colorful garments, or clean modern washing machines
   - **Size**: 500x400px (minimum)

3. **CTA Section Image** (currently using `/public/laundry1.jpg`)
   - **Suggestion**: Customer receiving fresh laundry delivery
   - **Colors**: Bright, cheerful atmosphere
   - **Example**: Smiling delivery person with neatly packed colorful clothes
   - **Size**: 500x350px (minimum)

### Where to Find Colorful Stock Images

1. **Free Stock Photo Sites**:
   - Unsplash (https://unsplash.com) - Search: "laundry", "clean clothes", "washing"
   - Pexels (https://pexels.com) - Search: "laundry service", "fresh laundry"
   - Pixabay (https://pixabay.com) - Search: "clothes washing", "laundry basket"

2. **Search Keywords for Colorful Images**:
   - "colorful laundry basket"
   - "fresh clean clothes rainbow"
   - "laundry service modern"
   - "hanging colorful shirts"
   - "folded towels colorful"
   - "laundry delivery happy customer"

### Image Guidelines

‚úÖ **DO**:
- Use bright, vibrant colors (blues, greens, oranges, pinks)
- Show clean, fresh, well-lit environments
- Include people when possible (smiling staff/customers)
- Use high-resolution images (at least 1200px wide)
- Ensure images are optimized for web (use Next.js Image optimization)

‚ùå **DON'T**:
- Use black and white images
- Use dark, gloomy photos
- Use low-resolution or pixelated images
- Use images with watermarks
- Use cluttered or messy backgrounds

### How to Replace Images

1. Download your chosen images
2. Rename them appropriately:
   - `laundry.jpg` - Main hero image
   - `laundry1.jpg` - About/CTA image
   - You can add more: `laundry2.jpg`, `laundry3.jpg`, etc.

3. Place them in the `/public` folder

4. Update the image references in `src/app/page.tsx` if needed

### Additional Image Sections to Consider

You can add more visual appeal by:

1. **Service Icons**: Replace text-based service icons with colorful illustrations
2. **Background Patterns**: Add subtle, colorful background patterns to sections
3. **Gallery Section**: Add a photo gallery showing before/after or different services
4. **Team Photos**: Add photos of your team members with colorful uniforms

### Color Palette Recommendations

Based on your brand colors:
- **Primary**: Navy blue (#0D3B66)

Choose images that complement these colors for a cohesive look!
